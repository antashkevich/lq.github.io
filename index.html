Zarina Tsomaeva

Founder & CEO

Zarina Tsomaeva is a globally experienced entrepreneur, executive & businesswoman in financial, oil & gas and hospitality industries.

Ms Tsomaeva held various executive, board and advisory roles in a number of multi-billion US dollars industrial groups. She is a former Executive Director at Goldman Sachs Investment Management Division & Director at Citi Group.

Zarina holds an MBA from the University of Chicago and Law Degrees from both The John Marshall Law School in Chicago and St. Petersburg State University in Russia.

Bill Gajda

Strategic Partnerships and Distribution/ Investor

Bill Gajda is the Global Head of Innovation and Strategic Partnerships at VISA. He is also responsible for Visa’s global relationships with Mobile Operators and leads the ‘Visa Ready’ and Venture Capital programs.

Prior to joining Visa, he was Chief Commercial Officer for the GSMA, where he led the GSMA’s Mobile Money, Mobile Innovation, Mobile Advertising and Strategic Partner Programs.

Bill holds an MBA from the University of Chicago and a BA from the University of Alberta, Canada

Jim McCarthy

Adviser/Investor

Jim McCarthy is the former EVP of Innovation & Strategic Partnerships for Visa Inc. where he was responsible for the development of Visa's product and technology roadmap, Visa's global innovation efforts, and leading global business development for strategic partnerships.

Since joining Visa in 1999, McCarthy has held several senior leadership positions, including Head of Emerging Products for Visa USA, Head of North America Financial Institution Sales and Head of Global Consumer Products. Jim led the development of Verified by Visa, an identity verification product that has become the standard for payment security and fraud prevention in eCommercee.

Prior to joining Visa in 1999, McCarthy worked at IBM where he served as the senior client representative to Bank of America. In that role, he was responsible for direct sales and account management including marketing, technology solutions and the development of joint business strategies.

McCarthy earned a Bachelor of Science degree from the College of William and Mary in 1986.

Ilya Galkevich

CTO

Over the last 15 years Ilya held various positions from a developer to a team leader and project manager.

Ilya has been responsible for implementation and deployment of ECM and other IT solutions for subsidiaries of Royal Dutch Shell, BP, ExxonMobil, Chevron, InterCall, Casino.com and other Fortune 500 clients, leading the teams of 100+ engineers.

Ilya holds a Master’s degree in applied mathematics and computer science from Belarusian State University. He is also a Certified Scrum Master.

Rob Currie

Technical Advisor

Rob Currie has over 25 years of experience as a senior executive at Silicon Valley early stage technology companies including Universal Audio, Dash Navigation/Blackberry, Strangeberry/TiVo, Marimba/BMC and Digidesign/Avid in fields including machine learning, signal processing, distributed systems management and geospatial navigation.

Rob received a BS in EECS from UC Berkeley with high honors and an MBA from University of Chicago Booth with honors. He is currently CTO of the UCSC Genomics Institute.
